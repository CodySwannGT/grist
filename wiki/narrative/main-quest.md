# Main quest — beat sheet

> **Spoiler-bearing.** The critical-path chapter outline for both acts. It
> sequences the mystery layers and party assembly from [story](story.md) into
> playable chapters. This is a **first-pass plan** ([gdd](../production/gdd.md)) —
> beats will move as the build teaches us. Side-stories live in
> [quest-design](../design/quest-design.md); the world they cross in
> [regions](../design/regions.md).

## Shape

~40+ hours, two acts across one transforming open world
([open-world](../design/open-world.md)). Act I ≈ chapters 1–5; the **Reckoning** is
the midpoint world-turn; Act II ≈ chapters 6–10. Chapters are *beats*, not levels —
the world stays open and the pacing stays unhurried ([overview](../design/overview.md)).

## Act I — The Reach (World of Balance)

### Ch.1 — The delivery *(Layer 0: the hook)*
- **Where:** the Marrow ([world](world.md)). **POV:** Wren.
- **Beats:** A routine smuggling run; the cargo turns out to be a person — a sleeping
  girl in a stasis-coffin (Sable). The drop goes wrong; House muscle wants her badly.
  Wren runs instead of handing her over.
- **Teaches:** movement, interaction, the first ATB skirmish, the grist wallet.
- **Gate:** escape the ambush → into the undercity.

### Ch.2 — Runner's debts *(Layer 1: the asset)*
- **Beats:** Lying low with **Tobi** ([characters](characters.md)), who stabilizes
  Sable's coffin and reveals she *produces* grist without being rendered — the Houses
  want her farmed, not dead. First serious pursuit by a Mourne **frame**-knight
  (Halcyon, still an enemy).
- **Teaches:** the growth screen, first augment, party of two.
- **Gate:** survive the frame chase.

### Ch.3 — What the city eats *(Layer 2: the supply chain)*
- **Beats:** Following the fuel back to its source through the Tiers and the Marrow's
  rendering-houses; the player *sees* the supply chain. Meet **Maren** the Withered,
  who speaks of an age before — and of something old, caged, nearby.
- **Set-piece adjacent:** the **vertical-slice** content sits here — *The Bound in the
  Marrow* ([vertical-slice](../production/vertical-slice.md)): the first Bound, the
  free-vs-wield choice, the shard.
- **Gate:** descend toward the Deep.

### Ch.4 — The requiem *(Layer 3: the original sin)*
- **Beats:** Into the Roots/the Deep ([regions](../design/regions.md)); the **Sidhe
  requiem-rite** set-piece ([story](story.md)) — a fragment of the Choir's Song, and
  the truth cracks open: the Sundering was *murder for profit*. **Halcyon defects**
  after confronting what black grist is made of, and joins.
- **Teaches:** Bound shard learning in practice; party expands.
- **Gate:** surface with the terrible knowledge.

### Ch.5 — The keystone *(Layer 4 begins; Act I climax)*
- **Beats:** Piecing together Sable's nature — she's bound to Aurel, the dreaming god
  ([lore-and-history](lore-and-history.md)). **Mr. Sallow** steps from the background:
  courteous, total, already moving. The party races to stop his plan — and *fails*.
- **Set-piece:** confrontation at a Mourne refinery atop Aurel's corpse.
- **Gate:** Sallow triggers the Reckoning.

## The Reckoning — the world-turn (midpoint)

Sallow's **Second Sundering**: he overloads Aurel's corpse-reactor and renders a
swath of the Reach at once. Lower Vanta and a whole region are liquidated; the party
is **scattered and broken**; Sable is taken/lost. The open world tips into
**Ashfall** ([open-world](../design/open-world.md)). Hard cut; the music and color
drain ([art-direction](../design/art-direction.md),
[audio-direction](../design/audio-direction.md)).

## Act II — Ashfall (World of Ruin)

### Ch.6 — After *(the elegy begins)*
- **Beats:** Alone in the transformed world (POV: Wren, or whoever the player held).
  The same places, mourned. Establish the Ashfall map and the new stakes: not *stop a
  plot* but *decide a dying world's fate*.
- **Gate:** find the first scattered companion.

### Ch.7 — Gathering the lost *(reunions)*
- **Beats:** Open, nonlinear **reunion quests** across Ashfall — each a self-contained
  story; each missable ([quest-design](../design/quest-design.md)). Meet **Quietus**,
  who reveals grist's nature from the inside (it is made of the dead — and so is Q).
- **Teaches:** the full roster, deeper builds, the Bound as open-world superbosses.
- **Gate:** enough of the party and the means to reach Aurel's heart.

### Ch.8 — The dreaming god *(Layer 4 completes)*
- **Beats:** The deep truth lands fully — Aurel's nine-century death *is* the world's
  entropy; Sable is its heir, not its battery. Recover/reach Sable. Understand what
  Sallow truly intends: not conquest, *completion* ([characters](characters.md)).
- **Gate:** the descent to the heart opens.

### Ch.9 — The audit *(Layer 5: the choice crystallizes)*
- **Beats:** Break the Concord's last defenses; confront what each faction wants done
  with a dying god ([factions](factions.md)). The ending paths become explicit; the
  player's accumulated standing and choices determine which are reachable
  ([story](story.md), [side-content](../design/side-content.md)).
- **Gate:** into Aurel's heart.

### Ch.10 — The last voice *(finale)*
- **Beats:** Confront **Sallow** at the god's heart; the Choir's Song is heard whole
  for the first time ([audio-direction](../design/audio-direction.md)). The
  ending-choice: **wake the god / let it die / finish the Sundering / the third way**
  ([story](story.md)). Resolution and epilogue keyed to who the party became.

## Pacing rules

- **No ticking clocks in the fiction** except the deliberate Reckoning beat; the
  world invites wandering throughout ([overview](../design/overview.md)).
- **Each chapter peels exactly one mystery layer** (or completes one) — never faster.
- **Reunions are optional**; the finale scales to the party you bring (FFVI tradition).
